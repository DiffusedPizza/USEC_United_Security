Horizon Watch

| Overview |

Operation Horizon Watch emerged as a critical mission for USEC in the dynamic landscape of Tarkov. Tasked with establishing a forward-operating base, the operation aimed to fortify USEC's strategic position in the region, providing an elevated vantage point for surveillance and swift responses to emerging threats. Recognizing the long-term implications, Operation Horizon Watch was strategically designed to solidify USEC's presence and influence in this volatile environment.

| Challenges |

Operation Horizon Watch posed a spectrum of challenges, spanning from the intricacies of geopolitics to the logistical complexities of establishing a forward base. Navigating the delicate balance between proactive surveillance and potential confrontations required a nuanced approach. The operation demanded foresight in addressing unforeseen obstacles, emphasizing the importance of adaptability and strategic thinking in overcoming the multifaceted challenges inherent to the region.

| Strategy |

The strategy for Operation Horizon Watch unfolded in phases, blending advanced reconnaissance with diplomatic finesse. Specialized teams were deployed for intelligence gathering, meticulously assessing local dynamics and potential threats. Strategic alliances with local factions were forged to ensure the base's sustainability and foster positive relations. The strategy underscored the significance of proactive engagement, laying the groundwork for a harmonious coexistence between USEC and the indigenous population.

| Execution |

Execution of Operation Horizon Watch unfolded with precision, as USEC operatives strategically established a forward-operating base. The process seamlessly integrated advanced surveillance capabilities with diplomatic outreach to local communities. Coordinated efforts ensured not only the security of the base but also the cultivation of positive relationships with the local populace. Continuous surveillance became the linchpin of the operation, allowing USEC to proactively address emerging threats and maintain a vigilant presence in the region.

| Outcome |

Operation Horizon Watch culminated in a resounding success, solidifying USEC's strategic foothold in Tarkov. The establishment of the forward-operating base significantly enhanced surveillance capabilities, enabling USEC to respond swiftly to emerging threats. The operation's success was not only measured in strategic positioning but also in the diplomatic acumen displayed, fostering positive engagement and setting a standard for responsible private military operations in the region.

| Client Testimonial |

Local authorities lauded USEC for Operation Horizon Watch, expressing, "This operation showcased USEC's commitment to securing strategic positions while fostering positive engagement with the local community. Their diplomatic approach sets a standard for private military operations in our region."

| Key Learnings |

Operation Horizon Watch provided invaluable insights into the intricacies of strategic positioning in Tarkov. The operation underscored the significance of community engagement, the establishment of strategic alliances, and proactive surveillance in achieving a sustainable and effective forward-operating presence in this complex and dynamic environment.
